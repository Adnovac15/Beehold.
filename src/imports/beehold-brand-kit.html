VOICE & TONE
Straight talking
Beeholdern honest
+
WE DO
  • Talk straight. No flannel, no faff. Beeholdern honest.
  • Sound like someone from here — warm but no-nonsense
  • Back up everything with specifics: dates, numbers, results
  • Keep it short. Mancunians don't have time for waffle.
  • Show pride in the work without being arrogant about it
—
WE DON'T
  • Use London agency speak. We're not from London.
  • Overcomplicate what's simple. Call a video a video.
  • Sound like every other studio. We've got a point of view.
  • Punch down. This city lifts people up.
  • Chase trends. We set them.
— Before (agency fluff)
+ After (straight talking)
"We are a creative motion design agency specialising in the conceptualisation and production of high-impact visual narratives for forward-thinking brands across the hospitality and retail sectors."
"We make videos for Manchester businesses that people actually stop to watch. Restaurants, shops, venues — if you want more people through the door, we'll make it happen. Simple as."
